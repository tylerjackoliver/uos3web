ELECTRONICS GDP

 

In the academic year 2014-15, the School of electronics and computer science (ECS) at the University of Southampton ran a Group Design Project (GDP) as part of the UoS3 project.

 

GDPs involve undergraduate students who are in their Masters (fourth) year at University.  The ECS GDP was tasked with development of the payload and the data handling unit for the UoS3 spacecraft. There are five members of the ECS GDP:

 

  • Ben Andrews
  • James Bantock
  • John Blackwood
  • Lewis Cooper
  • William Hector
